# HR Management System - Architecture Overview

## What is This System?

The HR Management System (HRMS) is a modern, secure platform that helps organizations manage their employees, payroll, and daily HR operations efficiently.

## How It Works - Simple View

### The Main Components

**1. User Interface (What You See)**
* **Web Dashboard** - Your main control center
* **Mobile Friendly** - Works on phones and tablets
* **Easy-to-Use Forms** - Simple data entry and management

**2. The Brain (Behind the Scenes)**
* **Smart Security** - Keeps your data safe and private
* **Automatic Calculations** - Payroll, taxes, and benefits
* **Real-Time Updates** - See changes instantly

**3. Data Storage (Your Information)**
* **Secure Database** - All employee and company data
* **Backup Systems** - Your information is always safe
* **Fast Access** - Quick retrieval of any information

## Who Uses What?

### Super Admin (System Manager)
* **Access**: Everything across all organizations
* **Tasks**: System setup, company management, user support
* **Tools**: Advanced admin dashboard, system reports

### Organization Admin (Company Manager)
* **Access**: Your company and employees only
* **Tasks**: Employee management, payroll processing, policy setup
* **Tools**: Employee dashboard, payroll system, reports

### Manager (Team Leader)
* **Access**: Your team members
* **Tasks**: Approve requests, team scheduling, performance reviews
* **Tools**: Team dashboard, approval workflows, team reports

### Employee (Staff Member)
* **Access**: Your personal information
* **Tasks**: View payslips, request leave, update profile
* **Tools**: Personal dashboard, leave requests, document access

## Key Features

### Employee Management
* **Complete Employee Profiles** - All employee information in one place
* **Document Storage** - Secure storage for contracts, certificates, etc.
* **Organization Structure** - Departments, teams, and reporting lines
* **Employment History** - Track promotions, salary changes, and performance

### Payroll System
* **Automatic Salary Calculation** - No more manual calculations
* **Tax Deductions** - Automatic tax calculations and compliance
* **Benefits Management** - Health insurance, retirement, other benefits
* **Payslip Generation** - Professional payslips for all employees

### Leave Management
* **Online Leave Requests** - Employees request leave online
* **Manager Approval** - Quick approval workflows
* **Leave Balance Tracking** - Automatic balance calculations
* **Policy Compliance** - Enforces company leave policies

### Time & Attendance
* **Clock In/Out** - Easy time tracking
* **Work Hour Calculations** - Automatic overtime and attendance
* **Scheduling** - Flexible work schedules
* **Attendance Reports** - Detailed attendance analytics
